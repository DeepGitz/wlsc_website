Privacy Policy

Introduction

WLSC Consulting Pte. Ltd. respects the privacy of its clients and partners. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect personal and corporate information provided to us during business interactions.

Information We Collect

Information collected may include contact details, business requirements, and project-related data provided by the client. This information is used solely for the purpose of delivering consulting services, communicating with clients, and fulfilling contractual obligations.

How We Use Your Information

Any personal or corporate information collected during business interactions is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Delivering consulting, software development, and data science services as agreed with the client.
  • Communicating with clients regarding project progress, deliverables, and invoicing.
  • Fulfilling contractual and legal obligations.

Data Sharing

WLSC Consulting Pte. Ltd. does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties except where required by law or where necessary to deliver the agreed services.

Data Protection

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect client information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. These measures include secure storage of digital files, access controls, and regular review of security practices.

Data Retention

Client information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Upon completion of an engagement, project data is retained for a reasonable period to support any follow-up queries, after which it is securely deleted.

Your Rights

Clients may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data held by WLSC Consulting Pte. Ltd. by contacting us at info@wlscconsulting.com. We will respond to such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to This Policy

WLSC Consulting Pte. Ltd. reserves the right to update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@wlscconsulting.com.
