Data Science, Machine Learning, and Software Consulting

We help businesses make better decisions through data. Our team works with companies across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia to build reliable data pipelines, deploy machine learning models, and deliver software that solves real problems.

Who We Are

WLSC Consulting is a Singapore-based consulting firm specialising in data science, machine learning, and software engineering. We work with startups, scale-ups, and established enterprises to turn complex technical challenges into measurable business outcomes.

Since our founding, we have supported clients across a range of industries — from fintech and e-commerce to logistics and healthcare. Our engagements typically involve close collaboration with client teams, ensuring that solutions are not only technically sound but also practical to maintain and scale.

We take a structured, evidence-based approach to every project. Whether it is building a recommendation engine, automating a reporting workflow, or designing a data warehouse from scratch, we focus on delivering work that holds up in production — not just in a slide deck.

Our Services

We offer end-to-end consulting across the data and software lifecycle.

Data Science and Analytics

From exploratory analysis to production dashboards, we help organisations understand their data and act on it. We work with structured and unstructured data sources to surface insights that drive strategy and operations.

Machine Learning Development

We design, train, and deploy machine learning models tailored to specific business needs — including forecasting, classification, natural language processing, and anomaly detection. All models are built with reproducibility and monitoring in mind.

Software Engineering and System Design

We build backend services, APIs, and internal tools that are clean, tested, and ready for production. Our engineering work follows modern best practices including version control, CI/CD, and infrastructure-as-code.

Data Infrastructure and Integration

We architect and implement data pipelines, ETL workflows, and cloud-based data platforms. Whether you need to consolidate data from multiple sources or migrate to a modern stack, we handle the complexity so your team can focus on outcomes.

Our Track Record

We have worked with early-stage startups building their first data capabilities, as well as established companies looking to modernise legacy systems. Our clients value our ability to deliver results without unnecessary overhead — we integrate with existing teams and processes rather than replacing them.

Past engagements include building real-time analytics platforms for fintech companies, developing ML-powered pricing models for e-commerce businesses, designing data warehousing solutions for logistics providers, and implementing AI change management strategies for organisations adopting machine learning for the first time.

Every project we take on is scoped carefully and executed with clear milestones. We believe in transparent communication and practical deliverables over lengthy reports.
