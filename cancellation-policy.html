Cancellation Policy

WLSC Consulting Pte. Ltd. provides professional consulting services in the fields of data science, machine learning, artificial intelligence change management, and software development.

All services are delivered digitally and are customized according to the specific needs of each client project.

Cancellation requests submitted before work has begun may be considered at the sole discretion of WLSC Consulting Pte. Ltd.

Once a project proposal or engagement agreement has been accepted by the client and work has commenced, the service is considered delivered on a best-effort basis. Cancellations after work has started will be evaluated individually, taking into account the amount of work already completed and resources allocated.

If you wish to discuss a cancellation, please contact us at info@wlscconsulting.com.
